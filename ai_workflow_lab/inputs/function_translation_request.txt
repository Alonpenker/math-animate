Topic: Horizontal and vertical translations of quadratic functions

Misconceptions:
- Students may think subtracting 2 inside the function moves the graph left.
- Students may confuse changes inside the function with vertical movement.

Constraints:
- Make the graph and its vertex visibly move while the axes remain fixed.
- Teach the horizontal translation before the vertical translation.
- Keep the parent graph available as a reference when useful.
- Use minimal on-screen text.

Examples:
- y = x^2
- y = (x - 2)^2
- y = (x - 2)^2 + 1

Number of scenes: 2
