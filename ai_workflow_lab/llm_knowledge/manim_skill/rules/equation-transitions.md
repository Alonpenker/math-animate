# Equation Transitions

Equation snapshots should make the mathematical change easy to identify.

- Align equals signs or central operators across related snapshots.
- Keep unchanged terms in corresponding semantic positions.
- Highlight only the operation or term that matters in the current snapshot.
- Keep the old form visible only when comparison or evidence requires it.
- Use a separate snapshot for each meaningful simplification or substitution.

For equations shown beside a diagram or graph, keep the equation panel in the
same layout region and preserve its overall scale across transforms.
